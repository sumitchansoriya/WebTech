---Game Rules---

                                       

Initial Setup

  • The rooks are placed on the outside corners, right and left edge.
  • The knights are placed immediately inside of the rooks.
  • The bishops are placed immediately inside of the knights.
  • The queen is placed on the central square of the same color of that of the player: white queen on the white square and black queen on the black square.
  • The king takes the vacant spot next to the queen.
  • The pawns are placed one square in front of all of the other pieces.
  • Milk


Gameplay

  1. The king moves exactly one square horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  2. A special move with the king known as castling is allowed only once per player, per game.
  3. The king and rook involved in castling must not have previously moved.
  4. There must be no pieces between the king and the rook.
  5. The castling must be kingside or queenside as shown in the diagram.

Roles & Moves

King
- The man in Charge can move one step in any directions.
Queen
- The Game Changer can move any number of steps in any direction.
Pawn
- Under valued yet Valued can take only one step forward.
